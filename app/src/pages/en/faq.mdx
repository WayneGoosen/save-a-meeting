<h2> What are the alternatives to meetings? </h2>

Depending on your organisation there might be prefferred ways of communication so follow those or suggest alternatives from below. Here are some effective alternatives that can help avoid a meeting:

- **"Do your homework"**: Sometimes the answer you need is present in documentation. Be sure to explore available documentation extensively before contacting the relevant person or setting up a meeting.
- **Email:** This is a great async way to communicate simple information or updates to a group of people.
- **Chat:** This is a good way to communicate directly or orchestrate the required individuals together.
- **Phone call:** This is a good option for more complex discussions or when you need to get a quick answer.
